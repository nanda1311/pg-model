Our Story

From One Person’s Struggle to a Home for 200+

In 2018, when our founder Sujatha Vekumalla moved to Bengaluru for work, she faced the same problem thousands of young professionals face — finding a PG that felt safe, clean, affordable, and most importantly, like home.

After months of disappointing experiences with unhygienic rooms, poor food, and zero emotional connect, she decided to build something better.

In 2019, New-SNS started with just 12 rooms in Koramangala. Today, we have three properties and are proud home to over 200 happy residents who trust us like family.

Our promise remains the same: Treat every resident with care, provide comfort that feels personal, and maintain the highest standards of safety and hygiene.

Home-Like Comfort
