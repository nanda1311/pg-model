Our Story

Built from
Personal Experience

When Sujatha Vekumalla moved to Bengaluru in 2018 to start her career, she struggled to find accommodation that truly felt safe, affordable, and homely. Every PG she visited felt like a compromise.

That frustration became the seed of New-SNS. In 2019, Sujatha opened the first property in Kasturi Nagar with just 12 rooms and a promise — every resident would feel genuinely cared for.

Today, New-SNS is home to over 200 residents across three properties and continues to grow on the foundation of trust, comfort, and community.

Sujatha Vekumalla Founder & Managing Director, New-SNS
